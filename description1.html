In this digital era where people are always striving to achieve the best in their life and also never stop planning ahead, they often forget to live in the moment. They would rather make plans way ahead from where they are right now than enjoying what they have right now. People are too scared that if they have no plans for their future, they will lose everything. In fact, if one is too busy planning ahead but forget to live in the moment, it does not benefit as well.
